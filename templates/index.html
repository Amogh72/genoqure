Fabry Disease

Genetic Cause

It is a rare X-linked disorder caused by mutations in the GLA gene.

Enzyme Deficiency

The mutation reduces or eliminates the enzyme alpha-galactosidase A.

Cellular Impact

Harmful lipids accumulate inside cells, leading to progressive organ damage.

Organs Affected

The heart, kidneys, nervous system, and skin are commonly impacted.
